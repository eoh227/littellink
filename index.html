Little Link

A Smart Marketplace for Children's Items

The Smart Choice for Growing Children

Little Link is a mobile platform designed to efficiently circulate children's items and help parents build a supportive community.

With age-specific categories and easy donation options, you can find and share needed items more easily than ever before.

Key Features

Age-Specific Filtering

Easily find products matching your child's age with our age range filters: 0-2 years, 3-5 years, 6-10 years, 11-13 years, and 14-17 years.

Simple Donation Option

Quickly donate items you no longer need with our free donation feature. Pay it forward to another family!

Parent Community

Connect with other parents in our community space where you can share parenting tips, experiences, and advice.

Secure Transactions

Trade with confidence using our trusted user rating system and secure messaging features.

Location-Based Search

Easily find items near you and arrange convenient in-person exchanges.

Event Information

Discover and participate in local events for parents and children in your community.
